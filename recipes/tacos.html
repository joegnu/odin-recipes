Tacos

A very average taco recipe

A weeknight favorite, the basic taco is a meal we all know and love! With as few as three basic ingredients you can have a great tasting meal in no time, any night of the week!

Ingredients

  • Corn of flour shells
  • ground beef
  • lettuce
  • cheese
  • onion
  • tomato
  • taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Chop lettuce, onion, and tomato.
  3. Brown your ground beef, drain fat, then add taco seasoning and 1/2 cup of water and simmer for 10 mins.
  4. Put taco shells in oven for 5 minutes while ground beef is simmering.
  5. After 10 minutes when beef is done cooking remove from heat assemble tacos and enjoy!
