Ham sandwich

In desperate need of something to eat!

Did you forget to set something out the night before? Did the leftovers you had planned on eating spoil? Then in a position of pure despiration this is just the recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Layout bread.
  2. Spread mayo on top slice of bread.
  3. Place as much ham as you like, this is all you'll be eating tonight after all.
