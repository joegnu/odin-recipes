Spaghetti

Easy 10 minutes spaghetti

A simple meal that is always there when you need it.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spagehtti sauce
  • parmasean cheese

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to a rolling boil.
  2. Place Spaghetti noodles in boiling water for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat up Spaghetti sauce in microwave or on stovetop.
  4. Drain spaghetti and enjoy!
